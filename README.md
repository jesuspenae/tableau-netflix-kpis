## 🔍 Main Insights

- **Movies** dominate the catalog at 69.6% vs 30.4% TV Shows.
- **United States** is the largest content producer by a wide margin.
- **Dramas and Comedies** are the most represented genres globally.
- Content additions peaked in **2018–2019**, reflecting Netflix's aggressive expansion phase.
- **Rating distribution** is skewed toward mature audiences (TV-MA, TV-14).
